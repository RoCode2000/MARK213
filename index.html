Understanding Our Market Share Calculations against Competitors

A brief overview of the methods used to determine market share.

To accurately assess the market share of Oral-B, TePe, and Pearlie White within the interdental brush category, we employed a comprehensive analysis that involved:

  • Screen-scraping Shopee to collect the top 10 sales data for interdental brushes from each brand.
  • Calculating the total units sold and the price per unit to estimate revenue generated by each brand.
  • Comparing these figures to determine the proportion of sales attributed to each brand, thereby deriving their market share.

This method provides a snapshot of consumer preference and brand performance within the online retail space, contributing valuable insights into the competitive landscape of dental hygiene products.
