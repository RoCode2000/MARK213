Our Project Flow

A comprehensive journey from concept to analysis

Our project journey began with selecting our main focus, the renowned dental care brand Oral-B, and identifying its competitors, TePe and Pearlie White. The subsequent steps involved:

  • Conducting web and screen scraping to gather sales data from all three companies.
  • Analyzing P&G's financial reports to estimate Oral-B's revenue contribution.
  • Visiting retail stores such as Watsons, Guardian, and NTUC for firsthand insights on product placement and sales.
  • Purchased and used the product to get firsthand experience on product pros & cons.
  • Compiling and calculating data to derive meaningful statistics and percentages for our analysis.
  • Coming together as a team to meticulously clean the data, ensuring its accuracy and reliability for our analysis. And ensure that we have a shared understanding of our work.
  • Collaboratively come up with marketing solutions and recommendations, grounded in the data we collectively agreed upon, to enhance Oral-B's promotion of their interdental brushes.

This meticulous approach enabled us to provide a detailed analysis and strategic recommendations for Oral-B, aimed at enhancing the promotion of their interdental brushes.
