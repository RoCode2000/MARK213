Partnership with Actor, Miles Brown to Help Kids Conquer Cavities 2Gether

Mar 23, 2021

What is the Conquer Cavities 2Gether Initiative about?

Conquer Cavities 2Gether encourages kids and parents to take the pledge to brush for two minutes, twice a day with a fluoride toothpaste. For every pledge, Crest and Oral-B Kids will donate up to 10,000 Conquer Cavities kits, including toothpaste, toothbrushes and educational materials, to children and families in need.

Oral-B Kids also partnered with Americaâ€™s ToothFairy, a nonprofit resource provider that has helped nonprofit dental clinics provide dental care to more than 8 million children nationwide. During this partnership, the Conquer Cavities 2Gether kits were also distributed to five partner dental clinics in underserved communities within the United States of America.

This initiative forms a crucial component of Crest and Oral-B's mission to ensure that all Americans have access to oral care essentials, education, and dental services. In 2020, Crest and Oral-B contributed over $2.5 million worth of products and services to promote dental health nationwide. Additionally, the brands have invested over $140 million in professional development for dental practitioners over the last decade, and have developed educational resources to simplify oral care education for parents, teachers, and dental professionals, facilitating the dissemination of crucial knowledge about oral health to children.
